Job Title: Management Information Analyst

Based: Hybrid. Typically 1 day per week in Leeds or Milton Keynes.

Reports To: Head of MI, Insight & Analysis

Salary: £competitive plus benefits

Job Purpose

The MI Analyst will play a key role in providing accurate, timely, and insightful information to both internal and external stakeholders, enabling informed business decision-making. The role will also be responsible for the production and delivery of routine reporting outputs, including data packs and pipeline analysis.

Key Responsibilities and Accountabilities

· Deliver accurate and timely MI reports and analysis for internal and external stakeholders.

· Produce routine reporting such as customer data packs, pipeline analysis, and other regular outputs.

· Ensure data is reliable, consistent, and presented in a clear and professional format.

· Support the continuous improvement of MI processes and reporting outputs.

· Work collaboratively with colleagues across the business to ensure MI needs are understood and met.

Skills, Knowledge and Experience

Essential

· Good communication skills, written and verbal

· Reasonable foundation in mathematics and comfortable performing calculations

· Excellent problem solver & organised with attention to detail

· Knowledge of the constraints in handling sensitive and confidential data

· Experience of successfully and routinely delivering to tight and shifting deadlines

· Adaptable approach to work. Conscientious, committed, enthusiastic with a willingness to learn

· Able to independently understand stakeholder requirements and appropriately prioritise workloads

Desirable

· Experience reporting from the Acturis Broker System.

· Experience using Power BI or equivalent business intelligence/reporting software.

· Previous experience producing MI and presenting in a clear manner to stakeholders

· Strong MS Excel Skills (nested ifs, arrays. Working knowledge of PowerPivot and PowerQuery).

· Experience in cleaning, enriching and marrying large non composite data sets from various sources. Knowledge and experience using AI tools and plug ins to enrich data is desirable
