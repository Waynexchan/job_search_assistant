About The Role

Market Analyst

Full-Time, Permanent

London or Seething, Hybrid working

The Role

As part of our commitment to innovation, a core pillar of James Fisher, we have created an exciting new Market Analyst role within our Maritime Transport division. This position will play a key role in advancing our understanding of core markets, developing market reports, maximising value from existing systems and data sources, and strengthening market intelligence to support strategic decision-making.

You will provide critical commercial insight, translating global market trends into clear, data-driven recommendations. Acting as a bridge between market intelligence and internal strategy, you will help ensure our decisions are proactive, informed, and aligned with long-term growth ambitions.

What You'll Be Doing

Support the development of frameworks to gather market intelligence, competitor data, and industry trends. 
Ensure accuracy and consistency of market data for reliable insights. 
Monitor market shifts and competitor positioning, highlighting risks and opportunities. 
Collaborate with stakeholders to deliver tailored market insights aligned to strategy. 
Maintain processes to size key markets and support strategy and product development. 
Organise and maintain market intelligence systems and research repositories. 
Transform data into actionable insights, supporting dashboards, KPIs, and monthly reporting. 
Identify opportunities to streamline research and automate data collection. 
Assist with data integration during system upgrades or strategic changes. 
Track industry, economic, and geopolitical developments. 
Contribute to internal briefings and promote data-driven decision-making. 
Ensure all research activities comply with data protection regulations (e.g. GDPR).

What We're Looking For

Bachelor’s degree in Economics or a related field 
Experience with reporting tools (e.g. Power BI) 
Proficiency in SQL and relational databases (e.g. PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQL Server) 
Exposure to statistical tools (e.g. Python or R) 
Understanding of data governance, privacy, and security (e.g. GDPR) 
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills 
Excellent communication skills for varied audiences 
Ability to work independently and in a fast-paced team environment 
Proactive mindset with a willingness to learn and improve 
Enthusiasm for using data and technology to drive smarter decisions

Why join us?

Opportunities for career growth and visibility across the organisation 
A supportive, inclusive culture that values your ideas and contributions 
Work that truly makes a difference in safety, innovation, and global capability
