About the Role

Castell Wealth Management is looking for a Data Analyst to take ownership of KPI tracking and reporting across the business. Specifically, this role looks at taking charge of and improving existing MI reporting, with the aim of giving advisers and management clearer, more consistent visibility of performance and compliance metrics.

The role is split across three main areas: monitoring adviser performance against pre-defined targets, supporting compliance processes through data tracking and flagging, and carrying out ad hoc analysis to surface opportunities across the business. The candidate will also be expected to ensure that the available data is updated and validated regularly.

Key Responsibilities

KPI Tracking & Reporting

Build, maintain, and refine dashboards and reports tracking key performance indicators across the business.
Monitor adviser performance against agreed targets and produce regular reporting for management.
Identify trends, bottlenecks, and outliers in performance data, and present findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders.
Compliance Process Support

Track adherence to internal compliance processes using existing data sources and flag any gaps or deviations.
Maintain accurate records of compliance-related metrics to support audit and regulatory reporting needs.
Ad Hoc Analysis

Carry out ad hoc data analysis requests to investigate specific business questions as they arise.
Proactively look for patterns in existing data that point to commercial or operational opportunities.
Support wider reporting projects (e.g. AUM, retention, and inflow analysis) as required.
