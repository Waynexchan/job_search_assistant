About the job
Junior Finance Analyst | FP&A | London (Hybrid) | £40,000

Join the FP&A team of a fast-growing, independent B2B energy supplier as a Junior Finance Analyst — reporting into the FP&A Manager.
You'll get real business partnering exposure early: monthly meetings with internal teams, building relationships across departments, and delivering financial analysis that actually influences decisions — not just reporting numbers.


What you'll do:

Support financial performance forecasts and gross margin analysis across commercial customers
Investigate variances through strong reconciliation work
Build cross-departmental relationships to embed financial awareness
Support month-end/year-end processes and ad hoc external reporting


What you'll need:

Strong Excel skills, comfortable with large data sets
Financial analysis/forecasting experience
Sharp attention to detail
FP&A background and commercial awareness a plus, not essential


Hybrid — London
Permanent contract
