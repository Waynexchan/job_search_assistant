About the job
Location: London – Hybrid, with approximately 2–3 days per month in the office


Our Team: How we enrich everyday life 

You’ll be joining our Data and Decision Sciences (DDS) team at the heart of Bauer Media Audio. We’re a collaborative, cross-functional team working across nine European markets, turning data into actionable insights that drive business growth, improve audience engagement, and enhance operational efficiency.
We partner closely with teams across product, commercial, marketing, and finance, ensuring data is not just a support function, but a key driver of strategic decision-making.


The Difference you will make 

As a Data Analyst, you’ll play a key role in helping the business unlock the value of its data, particularly within our competitions and revenue streams. You’ll turn complex datasets into clear, meaningful insights that influence decision-making and deliver measurable impact.
You’ll work with stakeholders across the business to improve performance, identify opportunities, and build scalable solutions that support a growing data-driven culture.


Your role 

You will analyse performance and revenue data, delivering insights and building reporting solutions that support decision-making across the business.
Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
Analyse revenue, market trends, and operational performance to identify opportunities and risks
Create and maintain reporting for competitions data, enabling self-serve analytics across markets
Build and develop dashboards using tools such as Tableau
Present insights and recommendations clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders
Automate reporting processes and improve data workflows
Translate business questions into analytical approaches and actionable outputs
Support A/B testing, targeting strategies, and ROI/pricing analysis
Work cross-functionally with product, marketing, and commercial teams


The Skills you will bring 

Strong analytical skills with experience in data analysis and reporting
Proficiency in SQL for data extraction, querying, and manipulation
Experience with data visualisation tools such as Power BI or Tableau
Strong Excel skills (e.g. pivot tables, lookups, data modelling)
Ability to interpret complex datasets and translate them into clear, actionable insights
Experience working with large, structured and unstructured datasets
Understanding of data modelling, data quality, and data validation techniques
Experience using Python or R for data analysis (desirable but not essential)
Strong stakeholder management and communication skills, with the ability to present to non-technical audiences
Experience working with operational, commercial, or revenue data


Desirable: 

Experience in media, audio, or digital environments
Knowledge of A/B testing, experimentation, or statistical analysis
Familiarity with cloud data platforms (e.g. Snowflake, BigQuery, Azure)
Experience contributing to self-serve analytics environments


About Bauer Media Group

We are a media business focused on creating content that matters to millions of people across Europe. Our offering extends from print and online publishing to audio broadcasting and entertainment, alongside investments in other media related sectors. With more than 500 million copies sold each year, we are one of Europe’s largest Publishers. From women’s and celebrities’ magazines to TV listings to food and special interest, we own some of the most popular publishing brands in Germany, UK, Poland and France – both digital and print. But not only that. Reaching over 61 million listeners weekly, we operate over 150 radio and podcast brands in nine countries, spanning the UK, Ireland, Poland, Slovakia, Denmark, Sweden, Finland, Norway and Portugal. Family-owned in the 5th generation, Bauer Media focuses on the long-term, with a consumer-first mindset that guides us across our diverse portfolio. Our workforce of 12,000 shares a common purpose: to deliver content and services that enrich people‘s everyday lives.
